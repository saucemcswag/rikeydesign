INFORMATION


Jordan Terrell Carti (AKA Playboi Carti) was born
in Atlanta, Georgia, is an influential rapper known
for blending trap, punk, and cloud rap. He gained
fame with his 2017 self-titled mixtape, featuring
hits like "Magnolia" and "wokeuplikethis*," and
solidified his place in hip-hop with his 2018
album Die Lit. His 2020 album Whole Lotta Red
debuted at number one on the Billboard 200.
Known for his minimalist production and "baby voice"
vocal style, Playboi Carti has become a cultural
icon with his distinctive fashion and public persona,
shaping modern rap music.
